Meet the Instructions

Gregory Vasquez

Gregory Vasquez is from Southern California. Greg is extremely passionate in the marching arts and enjoys helping others reach their marching goals. He has had experience teaching at all levels, from Scholastic A all the way to Independent World. Greg has marched with Orange County Independent in 2013 where he then moved on to RCC Indoor Percussion from 2015-2017. For DCI Greg has been a part of Pacific Crest 2012-2013 and The Concord Blue Devils 2014-2016. Greg is excited to join the Instinct ensemble as a Bass drum technician to spread the knowledge he has acquired.

Naomi Bacop

Naomi Bacop is a part time bass drum instructor. She started her drumming career freshman year on bass drum then played tenors from sophomore to senior year at Bakersfield High school. Her drum corps career includes Blue Devils B in 2010 in which she played mellophone and bass drum in 2011 and 2012. She then marched the Blue Devils from 2013 to 2015 on bass drum. Naomi was a part of the Blue Devils’ 16th and 17th Drum Corps International Championship wins in 2014 and 2015, having received the Fred Sanford award for high percussion in 2015. Naomi’s indoor drum line career i includes playing bass for pulse percussion in 2013 and RCC from 2014 to 2017. She was a part of RCCs highest scoring winning show at the 2015 Winter Guard International Championship. Naomi currently resides in Anaheim, CA and works at an environmental consulting firm as a Project Geologist. She has received her Bachelor of Science in Geology and Master of science in Environmental Engineering
